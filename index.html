Wattpad is getting ready to celebrate a major milestone on our journey—ten years of The Watty Awards. Ten years of unearthing new voices. Of bravely forging new worlds. Ten years of discovering and celebrating the best stories on Wattpad—written by you.

The 10th anniversary Watty Awards kick off on June 4th. Are you ready? Across a decade of Wattys, we’ve celebrated the journey Wattpad writers around the world undertake to bring their stories from their dreams and into the lives of readers around the world. That’s why the Wattys are so much more than an award. They’re a celebration of what it takes to tell stories.
